About Sally

Sally Cole got her first sewing machine at age seven. By the time she entered High school she was making all of here own clothes. Sally opened her shop in 2009 with the focus on Women’s Alterations and Bridal. So how did she go from Bridal Gowns to Burial Robes? Before her mother’s passing in June of 2020, Sally promised she would make her mother a dress for her Special Day. It was a beautiful experience. Sally also discovered a void in the market and in the culture.
